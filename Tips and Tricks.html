Helpful Tips & Tricks

Tip #1: Explore Every Key!

In KEYSEARCH, every key on your keyboard triggers a new minigame! Don’t hesitate to press any letter and explore what happens.

Tip #2: Look for Hidden Lore!

The game is filled with secrets. Pay close attention to every detail, as you might stumble upon hidden lore and Easter eggs!

Tip #3: Share Your Scores!

Don't forget to share your scores with friends! Keep track of your best achievements and try to beat your previous records.
