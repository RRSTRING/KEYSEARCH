Frequently Asked Questions

What is KEYSEARCH?

KEYSEARCH is a fun and engaging game inspired by '90s browser games. It involves random minigames triggered by pressing letters on your keyboard!

Where can I download the game?

The game is embedded on the main website, and you can play it directly from there!

Is there hidden lore in the game?

Yes! KEYSEARCH has hidden lore and secrets for you to discover as you explore and play.

Can I submit fan art?

Absolutely! Check out the Fan Art section on the main website or visit the Fan Art page to submit your artwork.

What inspired this game?

The game was inspired by nostalgic '90s web games and Google's creative mini-experiments. It's all about fun and discovery!
